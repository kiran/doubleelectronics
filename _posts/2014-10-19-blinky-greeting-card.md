## Before you begin

### Materials needed

- Paper!
- Copper snail tape
- Coin cell battery
- some LEDs (SMD or stickers)

You'll be able to find most of these in the paper circuits box, under the electronics bench.

### Prerequisites

- None!

### Skills learned

- Series/parallel circuits
- working with snail tape

## Process

- tape circuits down to LEDs
